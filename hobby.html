What's Binge Watching?

according to Wikipedia it is defined as 'Binge watching is the practice of watching television for a long time span, usually a single television show. Binge-watching as an observed cultural phenomenon has become popular with the rise of streaming media services such as Netflix, Amazon Video, and Hulu through which the viewer can watch television shows and movies on-demand.
